Scan

Have you ever found a brick around the house and wondered which set it belongs to?

Have you ever wanted to have more pieces of a specific brick to complete your original creations?

With the Scan feature you can recognize any brick and interact with it in Augmented Reality.

You will discover some details of the brick, such as the name and the code, and which of your sets contain it.

You'll even be able to buy other pieces of the brick directly!

Collect

With the Collect function you can easily search your favourite sets into the original LEGO database and add them to your personal collection.

You can also view some interesting details, such as year of production and all the bricks they contain.

Let's tidy up your LEGO collection!

Build

The future of lego instructions is here!

Build your sets with the help of Augment Reality: just point at your construction to see the next step on it. MAGIC!

The app will guide you every step of the way. You can easily zoom and check the bricks at any moment.

You can also point at the LEGO logo to see a full reference model. You can build side by side or simply check your progress.

What? You prefer the traditional way? Don't worry we got your back! Traditional istructions are also available in PDF format!
